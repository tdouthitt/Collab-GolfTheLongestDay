Player Information

Would you rather support a geat cause but not limp home? Bring in more friends and "tag team" your way through the daylight hours. For pace of play we ask that only 2 members of your team play at one time.

A $200 registration fee per team reserves your spot on the course, and not just any course... The Dakota Dunes Country Club, designed by golf legend, Arnold Palmer.

Golfers will be provided a cart, breakfast, lunch, dinner and a sunset after-party on the Terrace! We ask that each team set a minimum goal to raise $1000 for the Alzheimer's Association.
