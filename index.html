The Longest Day - Golf Marathon

  

June 20, 2016 9:00AM to 9:00PM

  

Dakota Dunes Country Club, South Dakota

The Longest Day is a team event to raise funds and awareness for the Alzheimer's Association. Held Annually on the summer solstice, the duration of the sunrise-to-sunset event symbolizes the challenging journey of those living with the disease and their caregivers.

In honor of the Longest Day we are hosting an all day golf tournament to raise money to combat the disease. Put your passion for golf to work for good!
Make a team, set a goal, raise money and golf, golf, golf!

Would you like to test your endurance? Play in a two person team from daylight to dark! Do you have 100 holes in your bag? 110? This is the day to see.
